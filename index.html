Our Story

Rooted in Quality,
Grown with Trust

Dev Enterprises — operating as Nutswala — began its journey from the bustling APMC Market in Vashi, Navi Mumbai over 15 years ago. What started as a passion for bringing the finest dry fruits to every table has grown into one of Maharashtra's most trusted wholesale dry fruit businesses.

Every product we carry is hand-selected for freshness, flavour, and nutritional value. From California's almond groves to Afghanistan's raisin valleys, we bring you the world's best — at market-beating prices.
